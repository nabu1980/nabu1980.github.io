Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

There are a few aspects of challenge 2 (build a chessboard) that hinder its accessibility. First, the table that makes the board does not have any labels for its columns or rows. It also is filled with only 9 entries, which are in unique positions. This then leaves lot of empty cells with no information. Even further, there is no alternative text to describe what the table entries represent.
