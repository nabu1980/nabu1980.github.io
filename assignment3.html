Mayonnaise

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mayonnaise, colloquially referred to as "mayo", [1] is a thick, creamy sauce or dressing commonly used on sandwiches, hamburgers, composed salads, and French fries. It also forms the base for various other sauces, such as tartar sauce, fry sauce, remoulade, salsa golf, and rouille.[2
